Effects of AI in Scrabble
Personal Project

I conducted in-depth research into the effects of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in the world of Scrabble. My exploration covered key algorithms like DAWG and GADDAG, as well as the use of AI tools such as Maven and Quackle. I further delved into the performance of human players vs AI opponents, using data from the BasicBot on Woogles. This research sheds light on the evolving landscape of Scrabble and other games with incomplete information in the age of AI.
